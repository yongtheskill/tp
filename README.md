- **TaskNest** is a desktop contact management application targeted at university students juggling multiple courses, projects, and deadlines.
    - It helps users store and organise contacts efficiently, search and retrieve them quickly, and manage clutter as the contact list grows.
    - It is optimised for users who prefer a **CLI-driven workflow**.
- **Target user:** University students managing multiple courses, projects, and deadlines concurrently.
- **Value proposition:** TaskNest helps overwhelmed students make sense of competing academic commitments by providing a clear, structured way to track responsibilities, deadlines, and priorities in one place, reducing mental load and improving follow-through.

- For the detailed documentation of this project, see the **[TaskNest Product Website](https://ay2526s2-cs2103t-t17-2.github.io/tp/)**.
